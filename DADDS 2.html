Founded in 2015, DADDS Africa is a leading digital agency dedicated to transforming businesses through innovative technology solutions. Our mission is to bridge the digital divide in Africa by providing world-class digital services tailored to local needs.

With offices in multiple African countries, we combine global expertise with local insights to deliver solutions that drive real impact. Our team of passionate professionals is committed to excellence in every project we undertake.
